ASUS 60.5cm Full HD Pivot Eye Care Monitor: Specifications and Features

ASUS 60.5cm Full HD Pivot Eye Care Monitor, model VA24DQLB, is an impressive display unit that offers an excellent visual experience to users. With a screen size of 60.5cm (24 inches) and a widescreen aspect ratio of 16:9, this monitor boasts an IPS (In-Plane Switching) panel that delivers high-quality images with accurate colors and wide viewing angles.

The monitor’s panel is flat, and it has a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels, which is Full HD (FHD) standard. Its pixel pitch is 0.2745mm, and it supports a maximum refresh rate of 75Hz and a response time of 5ms (GTG). The monitor has a brightness of 250cd/m², a contrast ratio of 1,000:1, and a dynamic contrast ratio of 100,000,000:1.

ASUS VA24DQLB monitor comes with a range of features that enhance the user’s visual experience. It features Flicker-Free technology that reduces flicker to minimize eyestrain and headaches. The monitor also has a Blue Light Filter that protects the user’s eyes from harmful blue light. Additionally, it has an anti-glare coating that reduces reflections and prevents eye fatigue.

This monitor has built-in speakers and a headphone jack, making it ideal for a variety of uses, including gaming and watching movies. It also has a USB hub, HDCP support, and a pivot (rotation) feature that allows users to rotate the screen to portrait or landscape mode. The monitor has a height-adjustable stand that can be tilted and swiveled to provide the best viewing angle. The product weighs 5.5kg and comes in the size of 540×350~480x211mm.
